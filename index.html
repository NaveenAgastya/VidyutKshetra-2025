About The Event

Welcome to VidyutKshetra 2025, the flagship technical festival organized by the Department of BCA at Sri Jagadguru Renukacharya College. Drawing inspiration from ancient Indian mythology and merging it with cutting-edge technology, VidyutKshetra represents the perfect synthesis of our rich cultural heritage and modern technological advancement.

Now in its 1st edition, VidyutKshetra has established itself as one of the most anticipated technical festivals in the collegiate circuit, attracting talented participants from across the region. This two-day extravaganza offers a diverse array of competitive events spanning coding, gaming, business strategy, and creative problem-solving.

VidyutKshetra aims to create a platform where technical knowledge meets creative thinking, where competition fosters collaboration, and where students can showcase their talents while developing essential skills for tomorrow's technology landscape. Just as ancient battlefields determined the course of history, we believe that competitions like these shape the innovators of tomorrow.

What is VIDYUTKSHETRA?

VIDYUTKSHETRA is an intercollege IT and gaming competition inspired by mythological themes. It features events like coding challenges, gaming tournaments, treasure hunts, and innovation pitches.

The event is open to all college students. Each event has specific team size requirements, so check the event details.
